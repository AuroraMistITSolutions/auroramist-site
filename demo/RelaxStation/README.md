## Next steps

### Before the pitch
1. Open `index.html` on your phone and walk through it once so you're smooth.
2. Confirm Relax Station's **real hours** (the site currently uses placeholder mall hours: Mon–Sat 10–8, Sun 12–6).
3. Bring printed copies of the proposal PDF.

### Once they say yes
1. **Register the domain** (e.g. relaxstationtoledo.com) and set up hosting.
2. **Replace placeholders** — see the list below.
3. **Connect online booking** — set up Square Appointments (free, with SMS reminders) and link the "Book Now" button + booking form to it.
4. **Google Business Profile** — claim/verify and optimize the listing (this is the #1 local SEO lever).
5. **Apple Business Connect** + **Yelp** — create/claim listings.
6. **Submit `sitemap.xml`** to Google Search Console and Bing Webmaster Tools so they index the site.
7. **Add real photos** of the space and team to replace the styled placeholders.
8. **Set up the review QR code** linking to their Google review page; print the stand.

### Placeholders to update before going live
- **Domain** — replace `https://www.relaxstationtoledo.com/` everywhere it appears (in `index.html` canonical/OG tags + structured data, `robots.txt`, and `sitemap.xml`).
- **Phone number** — add the shop's real number to the `telephone` field in the structured data and add a click-to-call button.
- **Hours** — confirm and update in the "Visit Us" section and the structured data.
- **Photos** — add a real social-share image at `og-image.jpg`.
- **`lastmod`** date in `sitemap.xml` whenever the site changes.

## SEO notes

- The page already includes a meta description, Open Graph tags, and **LocalBusiness structured data** (name, address, hours, price range) — this helps Google display rich info and show the business in local "near me" searches.
- `robots.txt` + `sitemap.xml` are standard files search engines look for at the site root. They must be deployed at the domain root (e.g. `relaxstationtoledo.com/robots.txt`).
- The single biggest ranking factor for a local massage shop is a well-optimized **Google Business Profile** with reviews — prioritize that after launch.
