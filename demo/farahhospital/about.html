About Farah Maternity and Infertility Centre

Farah Maternity and Infertility Centre is a dedicated women's healthcare facility in Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar (Aurangabad), Maharashtra, offering complete care across obstetrics, gynaecology, and infertility management — all under one roof. For families across the region, it has become a trusted name for safe maternity care, expert gynaecological treatment, and compassionate, ethically guided fertility support.

At the heart of the centre is Dr. Farah Deeba Shaikh, an experienced gynaecologist and infertility specialist who has guided countless women and couples through some of the most important moments of their lives. Her belief is simple: every patient deserves care that is skilled, honest, and delivered with genuine warmth — and that belief shapes everything the centre does.

Who We Are

We are a women-centred medical centre built around a single goal — to support women through every stage of life with care they can trust. From a young woman's first gynaecological consultation, through pregnancy and childbirth, to the deeply personal journey of fertility treatment, our team is here to walk alongside our patients with expertise and empathy.

Our centre brings together experienced specialists, skilled nurses, embryologists, counsellors, and support staff into one coordinated team. This means our patients move through evaluation, treatment, and recovery with continuity and confidence, rather than being passed from place to place.

Our Ethical Approach

Care that respects your values

We follow an Islamic and transparent approach to fertility and women's healthcare. All procedures are explained clearly, with honest guidance at every stage.

Treatments we do not offer

In keeping with our principles, we do not use donor sperm, donor eggs or donor embryos, and we do not provide surrogacy services. We also do not perform Medical Termination of Pregnancy (MTP), as the centre is not a government-recognised MTP centre. This ensures care that honours your personal beliefs, cultural values and medical integrity.
