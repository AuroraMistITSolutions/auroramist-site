Accurate diagnosis, effective planning

A thorough evaluation forms the foundation of every successful treatment plan.

Infertility Treatments

Advanced, ethical fertility treatment

Modern reproductive techniques tailored to each couple's needs.

  • In-Vitro Fertilization (IVF)Fertilisation and embryo development outside the uterus, followed by embryo transfer.
  • ICSISperm injected directly into the egg — a precise variation of IVF.
  • Laser-Assisted HatchingImproves the chances of successful embryo implantation during IVF.
  • Blastocyst Culture & Embryo TransferEmbryos cultured 5–6 days before transfer for better outcomes.
  • Intrauterine Insemination (IUI)Top-quality sperm placed directly into the uterus to aid fertilisation.
  • Laparoscopy & HysteroscopyMinimally invasive diagnosis and treatment of structural causes of infertility.
Advanced Treatments

Specialised care for complex cases

Cutting-edge techniques for difficult and male-factor infertility.

  • DNA Fragmentation Index (DFI)Measures damage to sperm genetic material to guide treatment.
  • Micro-TESEMicrosurgical sperm retrieval when sperm count in the ejaculate is very low or nil.
  • PGT-AScreening embryos for chromosomal abnormalities before transfer.
Fertility Preservation

Planning for the future

Preserve fertility today for family planning on your own timeline.

  • CryopreservationEgg freezing, sperm freezing and embryo freezing (for frozen embryo transfer in the future).

Our ethical commitment

In keeping with our values and an Islamic, transparent approach to care, we do not offer treatments involving donor sperm, donor eggs, donor embryos or surrogacy. We also do not provide Medical Termination of Pregnancy (MTP), as the centre is not a government-recognised MTP centre. Every couple receives honest guidance and care that respects their personal and cultural beliefs.
